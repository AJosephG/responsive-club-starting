Welcome to the Eldritch Wing Dragon Riding Club

About Us

At the Eldritch Wing Dragon Riding Club, we embrace the ancient art of dragon riding, an esteemed tradition passed down through generations of skilled riders. Nestled in the heart of the Enchanted Forest, our club is a sanctuary for all who seek to bond with dragons and soar through the skies. Our members range from seasoned dragon riders to enthusiastic beginners, all united by a shared passion for these majestic creatures.

Riding Times and Locations

Weekly Rides:

Dawn Patrol: Embark on a serene morning flight with our Dawn Patrol every Sun's Day at first light. Meet at the Dragon Hollow clearing.

Twilight Flight: Experience the thrill of night riding under the stars every Moon's Day at dusk. Gather at the Starlight Summit.

Monthly Expeditions:

Full Moon Flight: Join us for our special full moon flight, a magical experience flying alongside the radiant moon path. Held on the night of the full moon each month, departing from the Ancient Ruins.
